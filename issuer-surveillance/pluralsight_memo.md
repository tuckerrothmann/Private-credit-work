# Pluralsight — issuer memo

## What the company does
Pluralsight is a technology learning and skills-development platform used by enterprises and individuals for technical training. It once looked like a lender-friendly recurring-revenue software / content business, but the case is important precisely because that profile did not prevent a much weaker recovery and lender-control outcome.

## Bottom line
Pluralsight is not the best **live hidden-mark-risk** case anymore. It is the best **historical workout precedent** in the list. That makes it extremely useful for calibrating downside expectations, sponsor/lender conflict risk, and the gap between recurring-revenue underwriting and actual recovery outcomes.

## Working view
- Hidden mark risk: **2 / 5**
- BDC bond relevance: **5 / 5**
- Recovery fragility: **5 / 5**

## Why it stays on the list
- highly instructive lender-takeover / restructuring case
- public BDC and ratings commentary made it unusually visible
- anchors the bearish side of software-credit recovery analysis
