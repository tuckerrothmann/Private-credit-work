# Coupa — issuer memo

## What the company does
Coupa sells business-spend management software covering procurement, expenses, invoicing, payments, and supply-chain related workflows. It can look lender-friendly because procurement software is often embedded in enterprise operations, but it still depends on corporate spending intensity and budget discipline.

## Bottom line
Coupa is an important comp because it sits at the intersection of mission-critical enterprise software and corporate-spend sensitivity. That makes it useful for testing whether marks are still too forgiving for software names whose products are important but not immune to slower customer budgets.

## Working view
- Hidden mark risk: **3 / 5**
- BDC bond relevance: **4 / 5**
- Recovery fragility: **3 / 5**

## Why it stays on the list
- large software LBO with public private-credit linkage
- procurement / spend software can feel sticky until enterprise budgets tighten
- useful comparison point against weaker software categories
