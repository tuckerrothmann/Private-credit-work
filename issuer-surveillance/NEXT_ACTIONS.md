## Current working thesis
The best signals are likely to come from issuers that combine:
1. meaningful private-credit relevance
2. public evidence of weakening fundamentals, refinancing strain, or restructuring risk
3. soft-asset / franchise-dependent recovery value
4. a credible path for stress to matter to visible BDCs or major direct-lending platforms
5. a reasonable case that marks may still lag economic reality
