# Risk Buckets

This file separates the issuer universe into the three categories most useful for the project:
- **hidden mark risk**
- **likely actual impairment**
- **historical precedent / teaching cases**

These are names where the key question is whether economic deterioration may be moving faster than current marks, public positioning, or general market perception.

These names may not be the best live hidden-mark-risk ideas today, but they are extremely valuable for calibrating how lender pain can emerge and how recoveries may disappoint.

### Core teaching cases

## Why this split matters
- **Hidden mark risk** helps us identify where BDC unsecured bond investors may be underestimating future asset-quality pressure.
- **Actual impairment risk** helps us identify where lender losses could become real, not just cosmetic.
- **Historical precedent** helps us avoid repeating underwriting mistakes and gives us pattern recognition for what comes next.
